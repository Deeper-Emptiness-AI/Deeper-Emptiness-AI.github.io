Features

Everything, by voice, chat, or touch.

Every feature works three ways — speak to it, type to it, or tap. Nothing is locked behind a keyboard.

🎙️

Voice-first companion

Talk naturally about your day, ask health questions, or just chat. Healthya remembers your context and follows up like a good companion — warm, patient, never rushed.

🍽️

Meal tracking

Snap a photo — AI reads the food, estimates nutrition, and suggests a balanced next meal.

💊

Medication reminders

Scan a pill bottle or prescription for drug info, then get reminders and daily adherence tracking.

🌅

Morning briefing

A daily start with weather, headlines, and today's appointments — plus weekly and monthly recaps.

💧

Hydration & exercise

Gentle water reminders and light activity tracking that fit into an ordinary day.

📅

Calendar & appointments

Add appointments by voice and see them alongside the day's plan.

⌚

Connected health apps

Optionally read sleep, steps, and activity from Apple Health or Google Health Connect — read-only.

🚑

Emergency help

First-aid reference and a one-tap 911 dialer — the app opens your phone's dialer and you place the call. If you choose, family can get an alert with your location. Healthya is not an emergency response service.

The family app

Close, without hovering.

A separate companion app lets a linked family member support an older adult — only ever seeing what the senior has consented to share.

  • Brief, read-only health summaries — never the raw conversations.
  • Optional generalized activity area — never a live location trail.
  • Emergency alerts when the senior places a 911 call.
  • Every share is turned on — and can be turned off — by the senior.

Privacy & safety

Health data, treated like it matters.

Privacy is built in from the first screen — with clear consent, strong safeguards, and no advertising business hiding underneath.

No ads, no data sales

We never sell or share your personal information for advertising, and never work with data brokers.

You control every share

Health sharing, location, and emergency alerts are each opt-in — toggle any of them on or off at any time.

Health-app data stays read-only

Data from Apple Health or Google Health Connect is read-only, never sold, and never used for ads.

US data residency

Data is stored in US-region infrastructure with encryption and per-user row-level isolation.

Built around US privacy law

Designed for CCPA / CPRA, Washington's My Health My Data Act, Illinois BIPA, and related state laws.

Delete anytime

Delete your account in-app or from the web, and your data is removed within 30 days.

Healthya supports daily well-being and is not a medical service or an emergency response service. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional medical care. In an emergency, call 911 or your local emergency number.
